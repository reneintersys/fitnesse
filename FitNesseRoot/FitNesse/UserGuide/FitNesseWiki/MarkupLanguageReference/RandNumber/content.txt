'''randomNumber''' generates a random value within the defined range and allocates it to a variable

'''Syntax'''
{{{!randomNumber variablename (min max)}}}

|'''Markup Text'''|'''Displays as:'''|'''Variable defined'''|'''Remark'''|
|!-!randomNumber number1 (40 100)-!|!randomNumber number1 (40 100)|${number1}|min = 40, max = 100|
|!-!randomNumber number2 (0 1000)-!|!randomNumber number2 (0 1000)|${number2}|min = 0, max = 1000|
|!-!randomNumber number3 (-500 0)-!|!randomNumber number3 (-500 0)|${number3}|min = -500, max = 0|
